About Me
Ruichu Yu is an art student from China. Her runway pieces straddle the line between modern media and traditional craftsmanship. She plays with color and materials and is passionate about transforming flat paintings into three dimensions, connecting the real world with the spiritual world. The harmony of natural elements and overall structure is her concern, and her work is often surrounded by landscapes and fairy tales.

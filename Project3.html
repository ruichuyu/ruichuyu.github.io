Growth

Traveling is good for keeping active. I try to bring the outside world in through my artwork, and I want my pieces to look like they could live or grow somewhere. Living with beautiful objects that pay homage to the natural world reminds us to slow down and helps us relax.
Growth
  • Back

It's a concept about growth in general, not just technical growth. According to the laws of biological growth, some things disappear and some things grow throughout the year. Each season is represented by cherry blossoms, lilies, laurels, and pansies, while sapphires represent the stars as well as time.

Machines have aided manual production as the form of jewelry making has changed due to technological advancement. Different production methods are tried to improve the accuracy of the pieces from the beginning of casting and chasing to 3D modeling.

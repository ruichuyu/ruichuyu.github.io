Ruichu Yu

My work has been featured twice at Rochester Fashion Week. The most recent one was 'Restoring Energy' in 2022. I also participated in a number of sales where my resin earrings and enamel necklaces were well received and snapped up.
I received her Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from Rochester Institute of Technology in 2023. Early in my career, I worked as an apprentice in several art studios. I specialised in surface texturing of metals, but was more comfortable working with composites. My resin earrings and enamel necklaces are collected in jewellery boxes by many girls.
